1. What types of products do you offer at ShopHouse?
At ShopHouse, we offer a wide range of products including clothing for men, women, and children, as well as the latest electronics like smartphones, laptops, and accessories. You'll also find stylish bags, jewelry, and more to complete your look!

2. How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website's "Track Your Order" page to see the status of your shipment in real-time.

3. What is your return policy?
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase! If you’re not happy, you can return items within 30 days of receiving your order for a full refund. Just make sure the items are unused and in their original packaging.

4. Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We proudly offer international shipping to many countries around the world. During checkout, you can select your country to see shipping options and costs.

5. How do I contact customer service?
Our customer service team is here to help! You can reach us through our About Us page on the website, or by emailing support@shophouse.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
